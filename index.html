New Parent Outreach Policy
Teachers are to contact a student's parent when any of the following occur:
  1. Student has a pattern of missing hw (3 missing assignments)
  2. Student has a pattern of missing class (3 unexcused absences
  3. Student has failed a major assessment
  4. Student is at risk of failing a class halfway into the marking period (class average of 70 or less)
